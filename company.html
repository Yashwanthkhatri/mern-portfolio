Build Together. Build Stronger.

We're a B2B platform built to simplify the chaos of building materials procurement - by replacing outdated, fragmented systems with clarity, transparency, and value on both sides of the equation.

Our Vision

To empower builders and suppliers through a platform that puts customers first, rewards loyalty, and drives real value across the construction supply chain.

Our Mission

Brickbucket is on a mission to simplify the procurement of building materials for SME builders by connecting them with vetted suppliers, transparent pricing, and loyalty-driven rewards.
